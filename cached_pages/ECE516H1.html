Course Code ECE516H1 S
Offered 2015
Title Intelligent Image Processing
Description "This course provides the student with the fundamental knowledge needed in the rapidly growing field of Personal Cybernetics, including "Wearable Computing", "Personal Technologies", "Human Computer Interaction (HCI),"" ""Mobile Multimedia,"" ""Augmented Reality,"" ""Mediated Reality,"" CyborgLogging,"" and the merging of communications devices such as portable telephones with computational and imaging devices. The focus is on fundamental aspects and new inventions for human-computer interaction. Topics to be covered include: mediated reality, Personal Safety Devices, lifelong personal video capture, the Eye Tap principle, collinearity criterion, comparametric equations, photoquantigraphic imaging, lightvector spaces, anti-homomorphic imaging, application of personal imaging to the visual arts, and algebraic projective geometry."
Prerequisites None
Co-requisites None
Exclusions None
